About Us

Under One Woof was founded in 2020, and we offer pet packages for first-time pet owners. With the pandemic seeing adoption of new pets surge, we saw a new market opportunity to help provide those first-pet owners with some help on preparing new homes for their fur friend. Our packages help to set up families, ensuring that they have what they need for their new friends to settle into their new homes. We offer things such as essentials - bedding and food, as well as treats and toys.

As a team of four, we have come up with the idea to prepare and sell packages that include most of the essential needs for new puppy parents and their families.
